Welcome

I am a Senior Research Scientist at Siemens Corporate Technology and a Research Associate at ETH Zurich, Switzerland. My interests are networked embedded systems, RESTful environments, and building an Internet of Things around them. I am a contributer to the IETF, a member of the Eclipse IoT Working Group, and the creator of the Californium (Cf), Erbium (Er), and Copper (Cu) open-source implementations of the Constrained Application Protocol (CoAP).
